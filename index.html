So you think you can lease

I have been a property manager for about eight years now and I have seen nearly every possible way of destroying a property; be it an apartment or just a room in a house, tenants can damage and destroy nearly anything.

I had a tenant who rented a room in our house once who showed up to our "fancy dinner party" with friends and family in attendance completely naked.
After a while, I became concerned because I heard a sound like water dripping or splashing near the entryway to our house. We had a toilet that sometimes would run indefinitely and it had once flooded the entryway two floors below it, so I ran upstairs to check it, but as I went to the second floor, there was no sign of the water or sound of running water. Upon decending the stairs, I saw the source was our (still naked) tenant was now urinating on the radiator just inside the door.

If you are thinking of owning rental property, remind yourself of this story and of the fact that this was only one night of my eight year tenure in the business.
